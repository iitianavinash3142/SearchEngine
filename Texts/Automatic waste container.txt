An '''automatic waste container''' is a [[waste container]] which is automatic. This helps prevent the bin lids becoming clogged with trash.

It has an [[Infrared|infrared system]] that allows it to detect human presence and open or close the waste container.

These wastes containers are mostly made of [[stainless steel]].
