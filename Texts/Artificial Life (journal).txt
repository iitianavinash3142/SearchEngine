'''''Artificial Life''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] that covers the study of man-made systems that exhibit the behavioral characteristics of natural living systems. Its articles cover system synthesis in [[computer software|software]], [[hardware]], and [[wetware]]. ''Artificial Life'' was established in 1993 and is the official journal of the [[Artificial life organizations#International Society of Artificial Life|International Society of Artificial Life]]. It is published online and in hard copy by the [[MIT Press]].
